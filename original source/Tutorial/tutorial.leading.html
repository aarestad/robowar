Leading Your Shots

You will soon find that you are unable to hit robots that are moving unless by pure luck. That is because the robot shoots where the enemy is, not where the enemy is going to be. Therefor you have to lead your shots. If you find a way to do it great! Send it here for me to put up. If not, here is one of the most common ways:

doppler a arctan aim + aim' sto

a= the negative speed of your weapon.

  • Missiles = 5
  • Bullets = 12
  • Stunners = 14
  • Hellbores = energy invested
For example:

Fire:
doppler -12 arctan aim + aim' sto
1 fire' sto
return

If you find an error please email me.
